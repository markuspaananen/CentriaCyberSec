# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 18.12.2025

Booking System respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect the following information:
- **Email address** – to display your bookings in your account.
- **Date of birth** – to verify your age.
- **IP address and usage logs** – automatically collected for security, troubleshooting, and improving our service.

## 2. Why We Collect Your Data
We use your information to:
- Verify age compliance.
- Display your bookings on your account dashboard.
- Ensure security, detect fraud, and maintain the proper functioning of the app.

## 3. Data Retention
We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services, or until you request deletion.

## 4. Data Sharing
We **do not sell or rent** your data. We may share it with service providers who help run our app (e.g., email services), all of whom follow strict privacy rules.

## 5. Your Rights
Under GDPR, you have the right to:
- Access, correct, or delete your data.
- Restrict processing or request your data in a portable format.
- Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise your rights, contact us at: BookingSystem@gmail.com

## 6. Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.
